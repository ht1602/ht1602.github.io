$\sigma-algebras$其实是个集合系，它保证在这里头的集合，不管如何做交差并补，随便做可列次，结果都还在这个系里面。这对运算的良定义是很关键的。

What is $\sigma$-algebras

X represent all possible choose, P(X) represents its power set.

empty set $\varnothing \in \sigma$, if $S\in\sigma$, $S^C\in\sigma$

suppose we have dices {1,2,3,4,5,6}, ${\varnothing,{1,2},{3,4,5,6},{1,2,3,4,5,6} }$du

对于可选集合X，${X,\varnothing}$是最小的可能$\sigma-algebra$ (minimal or trivial $\sigma-algebra$)，最大的可能含有$2^X$个集合

对于同一个可选空间X的不同$\sigma-algebra:\Sigma_a$的交仍然是一个$\sigma-algebra$。

但是并集并不总是一个$\sigma-algebra$，有时候甚至不是一个algebra，但是它会产生一个新的$\sigma-algebra$。

$$ \bigvee_{a\in A}\Sigma_a=\sigma(\bigcup_{a\in A}\Sigma_a) $$

Simple set-based example

the collection ${\varnothing,A,A^C,X}$ is a simple $\sigma-algebra$ generated by the subset A.

eg. X={1,2,3}, A={1}$\subseteq X$, $A^c={2,3}$

$\sigma({A})=\sigma({{1}})={\varnothing,{1},{2,3},{1,2,3}}$

如果对于一个sigma-algebra F， 它的子集$g\subset F$如果也是一个sigma-algebra的话，就叫做sub sigma algebra

$\Omega$ all possible measure, F sigma-algebra, $(\Omega,F)$ measurable space, $(\Omega,F,P)$ probability space.

If $P(\Omega)=1$, P is called a measure.

If $P(A)\ge 0$, P is called a signed measure.

Probability measures are also called probability distributions, or just distribution.

$\Omega\rightarrow X$, $g\subseteq 2^X$, define $g={(a,b):a<b,a,b\in \mathbb{R}}$

A function $X:\Omega\rightarrow\it{X}$ ($\Omega\rightarrow\mathbb{R}^k$)is called an F/g-measurable, also $F/\sigma(g)$-measurable.

$\sigma(g)$ is usually denoted by $B(\mathbb{R})$ Borel sigma-algebra.

Thus, random vectors are random elements where the range space is $(\mathbb{R}^k,B(\mathbb{R}^k))$, if $k=1$, random vectors are random variables.

If f is F/g-measurable and g is g/H-measurable for sigma-algebras F,g,H over appropriate spaces, then their composition $g\circ f$ is F/H-mesurable.

Borel measurable: $B(\mathbb{R}^m)/ B(\mathbb{R}^n)$-measurable functions from $\mathbb{R}^m$ to $\mathbb{R}^n$. All familiar functions are Borel and all continuous functions are Borel, including addition and multiplication.
